At Omelette & Waffle Café, Every customer is like family. We serve deliciously fresh breakfast and lunch dishes in a quaint, cozy setting.

Matt & Jen Amin have been the proud owners of the Omelette & Waffle Café in Plymouth, Michigan since November 2010. We serve fluffy omelettes, delicious waffles, fresh fruit crepes, homemade chili and a variety of salads and sandwiches.
